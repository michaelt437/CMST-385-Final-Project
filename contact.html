About Me

Hi! My name is Michael, and if you haven't noticed, I like to draw. Art is a very passionate hobby of mine, and it has been for years. It started when my friends in grade school had drawing skills and I wanted to join in the fun. We had competitions during lunch to see who drew the better creatures and monsters.

Fast forward to today, I've graduated from sketching on lined paper to trying to understand the fundamentals of drawing and painting, like perspective and lighting. I discovered artists old and new that I look up to and gain inspiration from. Now my greatest competition is myself, and with this site I hope to continue to improve, but most importantly have fun!
